Brief Introduction

Ms. Yi Zhang currently is a research scientist at the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R) in the Agency for Science, Technology and Research, Singapore (A*STAR). She received her PhD degree under the supervision of Prof. Su Rong at School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), and B.S. degree at school of control science and engineering from Shandong University, China, in 2014.

Research Interests

Smart Cities

  • Urban traffic flow management

  • Model-based traffic signal scheduling

  • Multi-directional pedestrian-flow simulation

  • Multi-bus dispatching and operation

Optimization methods

  • Dual decomposition

  • Evolutionary algorithm

  • Mixed integer linear programming
